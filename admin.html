Elena Rostova

Multidisciplinary Visual Artist | Berlin, Germany

elena.rostova@atelier-berlin.art
Independent (Guest Curator Program)

Rostova’s work explores the intersection of digital decay and organic resilience. Using a proprietary process of digital deconstruction followed by physical oil glazing, she creates large-format canvases that challenge the viewer’s perception of depth and materiality. Her latest series, "Obsidian Echoes," has been featured in major European biennials.
